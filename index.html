European Refugee Crisis Migration Routes

A dashboard to explore data regarding the European Refugee Crisis and the migration routes used. The graphs and maps can be clicked and interacted with to filter the data. Click the Animate button to watch the migration routes change over time. For extra help click the help button to the right. The data was kindly provided by Frontex.
